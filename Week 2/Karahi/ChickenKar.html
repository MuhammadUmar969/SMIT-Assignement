Chicken Karahi

Chicken karahi is a delicious and flavorful dish that is widely enjoyed in Pakistan. It is a quintessential part of Pakistani cuisine and is known for its rich and aromatic taste. Chicken karahi is made by cooking chicken pieces in a thick and tangy tomato-based gravy, seasoned with a blend of spices and herbs. To prepare chicken karahi, the chicken is usually cooked in a karahi, a traditional cooking utensil. The dish starts by saut√©ing onions, ginger, and garlic in oil or ghee until they become golden brown. Then, the chicken pieces are added to the karahi and cooked until they are tender and juicy. A mixture of tomatoes, green chilies, and a combination of spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric is added to create a robust and aromatic gravy. The dish is often garnished with fresh coriander leaves and served with naan or roti, making it a delightful meal that is enjoyed by many. Chicken karahi is loved for its bold flavors and the perfect balance of tanginess and spiciness. It is a go-to dish for gatherings, family dinners, and special occasions. The succulent chicken and the vibrant gravy make chicken karahi a popular choice among both locals and those who appreciate the flavors of Pakistani cuisine.
