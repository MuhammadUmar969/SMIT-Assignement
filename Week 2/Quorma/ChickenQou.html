Chicken Quorma

Chicken qorma is a popular and flavorful dish in Pakistan. It consists of tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy and spiced gravy. The chicken is marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked with caramelized onions, a blend of aromatic spices, and enriched with ingredients like cream and nuts. The slow cooking process results in a tender and flavorful chicken qorma that is often enjoyed with naan, rice, or roti as a main course dish for special occasions or family gatherings. Its creamy texture and delicious taste make it a favorite among Pakistani cuisine lovers.
