Malai Boti

Malai boti is a popular Pakistani dish known for its creamy and tender grilled chicken. It is made by marinating boneless chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, cream, spices, and herbs, which gives it a rich and flavorful taste. To prepare malai boti, the chicken is cut into cubes or strips and then marinated in a mixture of yogurt, fresh cream (malai), ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. The marinated chicken is left to absorb the flavors for several hours or overnight, allowing the spices to infuse into the meat. After marination, the chicken is typically skewered and grilled to perfection. The grilling process results in a juicy and succulent texture while also adding a slight char and smoky flavor. It can be cooked over a barbecue grill, in an oven, or on a stovetop grill pan. Malai boti is often served with naan bread, roti, or rice, and is accompanied by mint chutney, sliced onions, and lemon wedges. The creamy and aromatic chicken, combined with the subtle blend of spices, creates a delicious and indulgent dish that is loved by many. The creamy and tender nature of malai boti, along with its delightful flavors, makes it a popular choice for special occasions, family gatherings, and festive celebrations in Pakistan.
