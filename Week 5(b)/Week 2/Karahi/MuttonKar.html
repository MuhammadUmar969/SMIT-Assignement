Mutton Karahi

Mutton karahi is a beloved dish in Pakistan that features tender pieces of mutton cooked in a rich and flavorful tomato-based gravy. It is a hearty and satisfying dish known for its robust flavors. Mutton karahi is prepared by saut√©ing onions, ginger, and garlic in oil or ghee until they become golden brown. The mutton is then added and cooked until it becomes tender and succulent. A mixture of tomatoes, green chilies, and a combination of spices such as cumin, coriander, and red chili powder is added to create a delicious and aromatic gravy. Mutton karahi is often enjoyed with naan or rice, making it a popular choice for special occasions and gatherings, showcasing the deliciousness of Pakistani cuisine.
